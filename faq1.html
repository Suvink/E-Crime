Answer: The NJ Division of State Police posts a list of crime statistics on the Web.
Addresses and telephone numbers are listed online for all of New Jersey's prisons.
New Jersey law authorizes the Division of State Police to make available to the public over the Internet information about certain sex offenders required to register under Megan's Law.
To report suspected child abuse in New Jersey, call (800) 792-8610 (TDD/hearing impaired: 800-835-5510). The Division of Youth and Family Services has a list of other hotlines that may be helpful as well.
